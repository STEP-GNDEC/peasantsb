BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION

The special feature of the programs of Business Administration in Germany is the combination of theoretical-based learning and on-the-job training stages. The program is run in close co-activity with partner organizations. Thanks to its cooperative study structure, the Business Administration degree isn’t fit to provide students with a strong establishment in business theory, but gives them wide skill in business practice and involvement with specific specialty regions. In theory-based programs, students who concentrate on Business Administration in Germany learn how to carry out the new information in management tasks. The authoritative responsibility with a collaborating partner organization guarantees that the degree program of a business organization in Germany is well organized and can be finished within a generally short period. While program standards are set by the academic authority of BSEL the students get a living stipend from their organization. After three years of studies, the students acquire their Bachelor’s degree and are normally offered a situation by their respective education organization.

Business Administration Programs are presented inside 11 different business specializations. Kindly look over the following specializations if you plan to study Business Administration in Germany and request data for details:

  • Banking
  • Office Management
  • Industry
  • Insurance
  • Global Business Administration
  • Operations and Transportation
  • Land Management
  • Administrations Management
  • Tax collection and Auditing
  • The travel industry
