FAMILY REUNION VISA

A family reunion visa, often known as a family reunification visa or family sponsorship visa, is a type of visa that allows family members to join their relatives who are living in another country. This visa category is designed to reunite families by enabling close family members, such as spouses, children, parents, or siblings, to live together in the same country.

To apply for a family reunion visa, individuals typically need to meet specific eligibility criteria, which may include proving their relationship with the family member they intend to join and meeting financial and accommodation requirements. The application process involves completing an application form, providing supporting documents, paying a visa fee, attending appointments, and waiting for the immigration authorities to process the application.

Once the visa is approved, the family member can join their relatives in the host country and, in some cases, may be eligible for residence or permanent residency in the long term. It's important to note that the specific requirements and procedures for family reunion visas can vary widely from one country to another, so it's essential to consult with us for the relevant country for precise information and guidance.
